Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 9, 2025

Inbox IQ is a Chrome Extension that uses AI to help users extract action items from their Gmail messages. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your data.

üîπ Information We Collect

Inbox IQ only collects the minimum data required to provide its core functionality:

Google Account Information:

  • Your name, email address, and profile picture (via Google Sign-In)

Email Content (Temporary Access):

  • The extension accesses the body of the email only while extracting action items.
  • This data is processed temporarily and never stored or transmitted outside your browser, except to OpenAI's API for AI processing.

üîπ How We Use Your Information

We use the data solely to:

  • Authenticate your account via Google Sign-In
  • Identify action items in your Gmail using AI
  • Send follow-up emails when initiated by you

üîπ Third-Party Services

To authenticate users and access Gmail (with your explicit permission). All communications are encrypted and follow Google's security standards.

OpenAI API:

To extract action items from email content using AI. Data is encrypted in transit and OpenAI does not retain your data for training purposes when using their API.

Note: These services process your data only as needed to fulfill your request and are bound by their respective privacy policies and data protection standards.
